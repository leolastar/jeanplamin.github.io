About Me

I am from a very small town called Man in the west part of my country Cote D'Ivoire. Man is a beautiful city surrounded by 18 mountains. Growing up there was one of the best moments of my entire life so far. I had the chance to experience nature at it's best during my childhood.

Before I was able to graduate from high school my country was disturbed by a civil war unrest and our family had to relocate the political capital Abidjan

Resettling in Abidjan was very difficult for my family since we had to start from scratch, but with dedication and hard work we were able to get back on our feet.

I was able to graduate from high school, then spend a year in India before coming to the United States of America to continue my education.

My journey in the US started in Seattle, Washington which I call my second home. I had the opportunity to meet a very wonderful family that took me in as one of their own.

I would like to take this moment to commemorate my grandmother Doris Morgan who passed away few years ago. She was one of the most wonderful human being that I haave ever encountered who took care of me as one of her own child

I hold Seattle close to my heart as it is a very beautiful place to live in, although it has become very expensive.

Upon leaving Seattle, I relocated to Texas to continue my education and now I work as a Software Developper in Dallas, Texas after graduating from Texas A&M University in December 2015.
