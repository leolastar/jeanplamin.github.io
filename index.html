About Me

I'am from a very small town called Man in the west part of the country of Cote D'Ivoire.

Man is surounded by 18 mountains and growing up was one of the best moments of my entire life since I had the chance to experience nature at it's best during my childhood.

Before I was able to graduate from high school my country was disturbed by a civil war unrest and my family and I had to relocate the the political capital Abidjan

Resetling in abidjan was very difficult for my familly since we had to start from zero but with dedication and hard work we were able to get back on our feets.

I was able to graduate from high school then spend a year in India before coming to the United States of America to continue my education.

My journee in teh US started in Seattl, Washington which I call my second home where I have the chance to meet a very wonderul familly that took me in as one of theirs.

I would like to take this chance to comemorate my grand mother Doris Morgan who passed away. She was one of the most wonderfull human being that i had ever encouter and took care of me as one of her own

I hold seattle dear close to my heart as it is a very beautiful place to live in although it has become very expensive.

Unpon leaving Seattle, i relocate to Texas to continue my education and now i work as a Software Developper in Dallas, Texas
